What Is NFIC?

The Neigbourhood Financial Inclusion Center (NFIC) is a government-backed initiative in collaboration with AMBAN (Association of Mobile Money and Bank Agents of Nigeria), aimed at ensuring every Nigerian is financially included by making key services like NIN, BVN, SIM registration, and more easily accessible.

Who Can Become An NFIC Agent?

We are looking for reliable individuals or businesses with the following:

  • Basic computer literacy
  • A physical shop or outlet (brick-and-mortar)
  • Willingness to serve as a center for BVN, NIN, SIM registration, and other digital services
  • Commitment to financial inclusion and community development

Why Join Us?

  • Official government-backed initiative
  • High demand for financial services
  • Earn commissions for every registration
  • Be a recognized center in your community
  • Access to training and digital tools
